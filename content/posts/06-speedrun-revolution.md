## The Machine Assist

Speedrunning used to be about grinding against a wall for a thousand hours to find a clipping bug. Now, communities run physics simulations of the game engine on distributed servers to mathematically prove where sequence breaks are physically possible.

* The *Titanfall 2* gauntlet record was recently lowered by another 0.4 seconds due to an algorithmic trajectory optimization.
* Glitch hunters are treating game engines like cryptographic puzzles to crack.

This fusion of data science and gaming mechanics has created a spectator sport unlike anything else.
