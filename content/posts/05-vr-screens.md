## Seamless Vision

The barrier to absolute immersion has always been the physical limitations of putting screens two inches from human eyes. The new "OpticSync" micro-displays boast an absurd 8K per-eye resolution in a form factor lighter than a pair of ski goggles.

### The Specs

1. 144Hz refresh rate native.
2. Eye-tracking integrated foveated rendering.
3. 120-degree FOV without lens distortion.

With hardware this capable, the burden now falls entirely on game engines to push enough pixels without melting the GPUs pushing them.
