## Budget Versus Passion

The metrics are in, and the industry has flipped. While massive conglomerates spent half a decade pushing out safe, microtransaction-heavy live-service grinds, small indie teams captured the hearts of players globally.

### Why Indie is Winning

1. **Agility**: A team of four can pivot their design document in an afternoon.
2. **Niche Targeting**: They don't need to appeal to *everyone*, just deeply to a specific audience.
3. **Price Point**: Releasing at $20 instead of $70 removes purchase friction.

The tools are now democratic; the only differentiator left is raw creativity.
