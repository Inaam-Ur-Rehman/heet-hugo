## Standardized Universes

It used to be a point of pride to have a custom physics engine. Now, it's a financial liability. The latest iteration of off-the-shelf engines offers out-of-the-box volumetric fluid dynamics, infinite polygon streaming, and global illumination.

Why spend five years writing tools when you can license perfection?

1. Faster onboarding for new hires (everyone knows the standard tools).
2. Better hardware optimization due to massive engineering teams at the engine creators.
3. Access to global asset markets.

The only losers are the low-level engine programmers, who are quickly finding themselves transitioning into infrastructure roles.
