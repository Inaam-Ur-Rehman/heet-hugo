## Aging Capacitors

The 16-bit era is literally disintegrating. As capacitors pop and batteries leak, the physical history of gaming is in extreme danger. 

* The secondary market for CRTs (Cathode Ray Tubes) has seen a 400% price increase.
* Specialized hardware engineers make a lucrative living "recapping" 30-year-old motherboards.

> FPGA hardware emulation is the saving grace, perfectly replicating the electrical signals of dead silicon.

If you have childhood artifacts in a damp basement, rescue them now before the rot sets in.
